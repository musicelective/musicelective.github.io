WELCOME TO THE SOUND OF MUSIC

The Sound of Music is the second musical Springfield has ever done. Held in the 3rd of March, 2018, it was also to celebrate our school's 20th anniversary, hence various of other bits of acts were also performed, such as Korean dances, a tribute to Beyonce, and so on.

The musical was directed by Ryssa Patricia, along with the Assistant Director, Raymond. It was also produced by Callia Reyner and Jennifer Chance, who were assisted by Ashley Suganda. 

The main roles were played by Audrey Gunawan, who were casted as Maria Rainer, along with Theodore Aristo, who played Captain Georg Von Trapp. Other supporting roles were also played by Fionna Theonardo, who were casted as Baroness Elsa von Schraeder; Tiwagar, who played the role of Herr Franz; Andre Nathaniel, who played Max Detweiler; as well as the Von Trapp Children, who were played by a group of other talented students.
