About the Teacher

RUTH INTAN BINARI

Birthday: November 17, 1988 (29 Years Old)

Place of Birth: Balikpapan

Occupation: Music Teacher


WORDS FROM MS RUTH

What major did you persue in university? Music eductation. 

How long have you been interested in music? Since I was 5 years old.

If not a music teacher, what job would you like to have? Inspirator in education.

Is it hard to become a music teacher? Or a teacher in general, in Springfield? Hard? No. Challenging? Yes, because I work with students as well as other teachers, and we need to work with hearts. 

What made you want to have a job as a music teacher? I had a calling to be a teacher.

What's the worst thing a student can do in your classroom? The worst thing a student can do would be breaking a musical instrument.

How do you usually discipline them? I remind them over and over again, but give them my understanding at the same time.

Is it enjoyable to teach in Springfield? Yes.

What do you think about my course? I think it's fun.
