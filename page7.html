WHAT OUR STUDENTS SAID

  • "I was a very shy person at first, honestly, but music class has helped me become a more confident person through perfomances." 

  • "In my opinion, the class is really fun, especially because you get to jam out with your friends."

  • "Music class, to me, is just like recess. It doesn't feel like a class period. On the contrary, it feels like a break from all the stress classes usually give. Perhaps that's because I personally enjoy music, but nonetheless, I think it's definitely worth becoming a music student."

  • "It's a pretty chill class, especially for those who enjoy music. It's very practical, so it's quite fun. Our teacher is also pretty laid back."

  • "My favorite thing about becoming a music student must be just simply making or playing music with my friends. It seems very ordinary from the outside, but you really don't know how therapeutic and intimate the little action is until you've tried it. If anything, music class have helped me create a stronger relationship with the people around me."

  • "Wow. Honestly, I didn't even know half the things my friends can do. Before taking the class, I didn't even know that my friends could sing a tune, so this class has clearly helped me opened my eyes to a lot of things. I've come to realise just how talented the people around me are."
